General Notes:

CFMRI PROPOSAL REVIEW PROCEDURES:
Several research propoposals types can be carried at the center
1) Proposals related to Human Neuro (Brain) imaging will be reviewed by the Neuroscience Committee chaired by Jim Brewer. These studies are typically conducted on 3TW. Exceptions can be made on a case by case bases. Click on the Human Neuroscience Proposals button to submit a new proposal.
2) Proposals related to Human non-neuro (others than Brain) and Non-Human imaging which includes animal, tissue sample etc will be reviewed by the Bioimaging Committee chaired by Miriam Scadeng. Studies are conducted in 3TW. Click on the Bioimaging Proposals button to submit a new proposal.

  1. Fill in all required fields.
  2. Proposal will remain editable until you click on the Final Submit button on the preview page.
  3. Once the Final Submit button is clicked, the proposal will be sent to the committee for review no more editing can be done by the PI.
  4. PI can use the Proposal Status button to check on the proposal status.
  5. The committee might send some comments to be PI.
  6. PIs will be advised whether there are minimum reviews that can be answered through submitting a response through the showcomments page that can be accessed by clicking on the comments/response button on the proposalstatus page or resubmit.
  7. Once the committee approves the proposal, it will be checked to make sure that all required certification/other approval are on file and the accounts will be added for research time scheduling.

Specific fields notes:

Proposal Form-General Information Page:
  1. Proposal funding source, please select UCSD if only if you have a valid active UCSD recharge index num, an index num ex RAD1234 the first three later represent your department. All other PIs VA, Salk, SDSU should select non-UCSD.
  2. What to image, select small animals or Tissues/Samples (one has to be selected).
  3. Project Title: Type the name of your project.
  4. Abbreviated Project Title (6-8 char): choose an short name for project between 6-8char this will be appended to PI last name and used as your webschedule project name.
  5. CO PIs: List all co-PIs please type N/A otherwise.
  6. Project Contact Person: explanation for this field is on blue on the main form.
  7. Scanner Operator: explanation for this field is on blue on the main form.
Proposal Form-Operator Information Page: You have to fill in only if you selected your own operator

Fill in Operator first and last name, phone and email

Proposal Form-Billing Information Page:
  1. UCSD Index Number: only if you check UCSD as your funding source, please provide an active index number to avoid delaying your proposal approval.
  2. Provide your billing address.
  3. Provide Your financial person contact, first and last name, phone and email address.
Proposal Form-Project Information Page:
  1. Total Number of scanner hours: enter the num of hours you anticipate your project will require.
  2. Night Time Scanning: select this option if your project requires scanning between the hours of 11pm and 8am, an additional project with the prefix night will be created, pilot hours ratio differ for night scanning subject to change. Night time scanning requires prior committee approval.
  3. Please indicate expected start and end time of your project. Proposal Details:
    Write a 3 sentences summarize the overall aims of your study. How will MR contribute to your study?
    Answer the detail questions about your project, Biohazard, data analysis and any additional equipment, if you have inquires please email Dr.Scadeng @ mscadeng@ucsd.edu
